Vacation Suggester

Where On Earth Should You Go?

A webpage (using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and JQuery) where a user can answer questions about themselves and be given a vacation destination based on their answers.

The site has six survey questions. It offers a minimum of 3 vacation destination suggestions.Note: You are not required to take every possible combination of answers into account. Feel free to use very simple logic, or even ignore the user's responses for some questions..
